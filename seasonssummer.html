Summer

Summer is THE BEST. School is out and everyone is happy there are no classes. You can go swimming, eat popsicles, and go camping. The weather is perfect for going to the lake, going to the beach, and hanging with friends outside. In my hometown of California, I love the evening time of Summer where it is just about to get dark. The temperature is perfect and it feels like I am a kid again. Go Summer!!!!
