Bluehost

The first Web Host I researched was Bluehost.com.

Bluehost's starting cost is $2.95 per month, and renews at $7.99 per month. Bigger plans are normally $14.99, such as Choice Plus. The biggest plan you can buy is Pro, which is normally $23.99 a month. All plans come with a free domain name for the first year. Bluehost is known for being simple and user friendly, making it a good choice for beginners.

GoDaddy

The second Web Host I researched was Godaddy.com.

Godaddy has four different plans that you can choose from. These plans range from $8.99 to $24.99 . ALl of these plans include 24/7 security monitoring, a free domain name, and 125+ free applications. Godaddy has good credibility and is well known. You can be a beginner with Godaddy, as well as having multiple complex sites. Godaddy supports all users.

ipage

The third Web Host I researched was ipage.com.

Ipage is normally $7.99 a month, making it around the same price as the other ones I researched. I did not see other plans that you could purchase for other prices. They offer a 30-day money back guarantee, as well as a free domain name for a year. They also offer free ad credit for search engines. While you can host unlimited domains within your account, there is also PayPal integration with unlimited disk space.

Which one did I like most?

The Web Hosting site that I felt was most appealing was the GoDaddy one. I am a big believer in going with companies that have credidibilty towards their names. I need to know that the company I use to host my website is going to be one that I can trust. I also like how they offer 24/7 security monitoring for all plans, not just the more expensive ones. This shows they care about their customers.
