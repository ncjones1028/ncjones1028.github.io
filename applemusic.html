Spotify VS Apple Music...which is better?

Apple Music: $9.99 per month is Starting Price. Student account is $4.99 per month. Spotify is free for Starting Price. The student price is $4.99 per month as well. Price is fairly the same for student users, so it really does all come down to personal preference.
