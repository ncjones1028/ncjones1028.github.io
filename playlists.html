Options for Playlists

With Spotify, you can either make your own playlists or you can listen to other people's playlists. There are so many option. In addition to that, Spotify makes playlists custom to you. There's a "Rewind" playlist as well as a "My 20xx Playlist". This is one of the key reasons why I like Spotify so much.
