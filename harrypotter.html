History of the Series

The story began in June of 1997. 500 hardback copies were made, with 200 of those going to stores and the other 300 hitting libraries. It was hard for Rowling to even get the first book published. Many people turned her down because of the length of the story as well as the idea of the boarding school. However, once it got published, it was game over. Book after book until there was seven of them. Harry Potter has had over 500 million copies. This makes the series the bestselling book series of all time. It has been said that the only book that have been sold more times than Harry Potter is the Bible and The Red Book.

About the Author

J.K Rowling wrote the idea of Harry Potter on a napkin on a train going to London. She is 54 years old and is the most successful author to date. She helped produce 8 movies to go with the books, and that has made her very rich. Her current net worth is $1 billion dollars. As for her more personal life, she has 3 children and is currently married to Neil Murray. For further information, please refer to a different website.
